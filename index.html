Our Story

More Than Just a Café

For nearly 15 years, Grand Cafe UK has been the beating heart of the Forest House Hotel. We aren't just a place to grab coffee; we are a place where locals meet, families gather, and travelers find a home away from home.

We pride ourselves on consistency. Whether it's the rich aroma of our coffee, the generous portions of our Full English, or the warm greeting you receive at the door, our goal is simple: to treat every customer like family.
